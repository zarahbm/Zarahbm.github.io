Zarah Butcher-McGunnigle

about

Zarah Butcher-McGunnigle (b. 1990) is from Auckland, New Zealand, and currently lives in Melbourne, Australia. She is the author of Autobiography of a Marguerite (2014). Her work has appeared in publications such as Cordite, Fanzine, and Best NZ Poems.
